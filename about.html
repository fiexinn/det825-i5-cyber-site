What is I5?

Detachment 825’s I5 program combines Cybersecurity and Rocketry tracks to give students hands-on technical experience and leadership opportunities. Learn more at the official I5 website.

Cyber Track

Develop offensive and defensive cybersecurity skills through competitions, labs, and simulated operations. Gain experience you can put directly on your resume.

Rocketry Track

Design, build, and launch rockets while learning propulsion, flight dynamics, and aerospace principles. Hands-on engineering experience in a student-led program.
