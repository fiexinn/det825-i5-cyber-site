Join I5

I5 is a national organization within Space Force Delta 13 dedicated to advancing cyber and rocketry innovation within AFROTC Detachment 825.

Email Us to Join

Who Can Join

Membership is open to Air Force and Space Force ROTC cadets as well as students at The University of Texas at Austin who are interested in space systems, cyber operations, engineering, and national defense.

How to Join

If you are interested in joining I5, send an email to i5tombargesquadron@gmail.com including:

  • Your full name
  • Major
  • Affiliation (University of Texas at Austin, Air Force Detachment 825)
  • Expected graduation year
